The b and strong tags are used to indicate text that is rendered in bold font. The main difference between the two is that the strong tag conveys semantic meaning, while the b tag does not.

For these reasons, it is generally recommended to use the strong tag instead of the b tag whenever possible. This will ensure that your content is more accessible and easier to understand for all users.
